Ultimate Guide to Olympiad

2024.7 ~ ?

I started a project Ultimate Guide to Olympiad, realizing poor documentation for olympiad preparation open sources and my passion on olympiad community. Overleaf Latex documentation will be uploaded in both archive and below. (I use the nickname Lleeya in many online communities)

(Current State) Ultimate Guide to Olympiad program is on a puase state from 2025.11 due to other urgent works. Currently about 60% of aimed problems are recorded, while composition overall needs some retouch.

(Overview) I have ran an actual short lecture series using this documentation. Progress seems essential in number theory while the documents in combinatorics and geometry seems relatively fine.
